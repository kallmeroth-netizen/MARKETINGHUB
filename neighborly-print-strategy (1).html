Production notes

Lead times

  • Brief generic & seasonal items 6–8 weeks before quarter start
  • Q1: Nov 15  ·  Q2: Feb 15  ·  Q3: May 15  ·  Q4: Aug 15
  • Send to print 10 days before in-location need date
  • Collab specials briefed alongside generic — same quarter window
  • Approval required in print tracker before send-to-print is checked

Rules

  • Poster always mirrors the active postcard — one brief, two pieces
  • Both locations run separately — quantities and copy can differ
  • Special postcards are collab or seasonal-specific; generics are always in bag
  • 10-day est. delivery from send-to-print (auto-calculated in tracker)
  • Artwork file must be uploaded before approved checkbox is marked
