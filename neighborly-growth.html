04 · Channel resilience

Eight channels. No single point of failure.

Dine-in, kiosk takeout, web takeout, web delivery, curbside, third-party delivery, marketplace, and catering — eight independent demand streams. The largest channel is dine-in at 38.7%; the second is web takeout at 25.2%. Nothing concentrated, nothing brittle.
