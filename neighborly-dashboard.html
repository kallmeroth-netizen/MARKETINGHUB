Formulas key

  1. SPLH (Sales per Labor Hour) = Net sales ÷ Total labor hours
  2. Labor % of net = Total labor cost ÷ Net sales × 100
  3. % of net (any row) = Row value ÷ Net sales × 100
  4. CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost) = Ad spend ÷ Customers acquired
  5. ROAS (Return on Ad Spend) = Net revenue from ads ÷ Ad spend
  6. vs T4W / vs 6-wk = % change from the trailing 4-week / 6-week average
  7. Concept mix % = Concept sales ÷ Net sales × 100
  8. Beverage attachment = (Beverages + wine + beer) ÷ Net sales × 100
  9. On / Off premise % = On (or off) premise sales ÷ Net sales × 100
  10. WTD / PTD / QTD / YTD = Week / Period / Quarter / Year to date — running totals
